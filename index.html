A Life Simulation of the Three Cradles of Civilization

What to Expect

Brought back in time, this life simulation website will immerse you in the lives of average citizens in ancient civilizaitons. You can be a farmer in the Nile River, a scribe in Sumer, an artisan in the Zhou Dynasty, or anything else among those lines. Regardless of what you choose, we have made sure that your experience will be accurate and enjoyable, set within true chronological historical events.
