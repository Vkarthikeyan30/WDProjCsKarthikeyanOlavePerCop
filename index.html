Website Information

A Life Simulation of the Three Cradles of Civilization

Website Rationale

The Clay and the Crown believes that historical awareness promotes the value of life today. While ancient sites and museums exist today, we aim to give you an experience in history itself in an educational and insightful manner.

How to Play

To play, first pick a civilization and a job. As you play through, there will be a story for you to follow, and at times you will have to react or make decisions regarding the dialogue. Your character's fate represents what would likely occur if you were faced with a similar situation as your character.

Introduction

Brought back in time, this life simulation website will immerse you in the lives of average citizens in ancient civilizaitons. You can be a farmer in the Nile River, a scribe in Sumer, an artisan in the Zhou Dynasty, or anything else among those lines. Regardless of what you choose, we have made sure that your experience will be accurate and enjoyable, set within true chronological historical events. You will also be able to learn more about the respective contributions, cultures, traditions, and unique ways of life of citizens in every civilization through creative storytelling and immersion.

Featured Civilizations

Ancient Egypt

Focus on Nile farming, writing, monuments, religion, and public duty.

Ancient China

Focus on family order, philosophy, craftsmanship, farming, and service.

Mesopotamia

Focus on trade, irrigation, early cities, writing, and law.
